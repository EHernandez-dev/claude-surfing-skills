    Surf briefing · Mundaka, Bizkaia · Saturday 11 July 2026

    Go surf Saturday morning.

    🟢 Go, 07:00-10:30. 1.4 m @ 12 s NW groundswell, light offshore southerly, low tide pushing from 08:14 (0.9 m above chart datum).

    The left will be working from first light (06:19). The 12-second northwest groundswell that filled in overnight holds through the morning at 1.3-1.5 m on the Bilbao-Vizcaya buoy, squarely inside Mundaka's swell window. The southerly stays under 10 km/h until midday, grooming the face; by 13:00 a 20 km/h northwest sea breeze tears it apart, so this is strictly a morning call.

    Tide is the timer: dead low at 08:14 exposes the inside sandbar, and the first two hours of the push are the cleanest shape. On your 7'0 this size is friendly; the crowd will not be, so arrive before the harbour fills.
